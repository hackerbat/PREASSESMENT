1. Open aws console
2.Select ec2 under compute section.
3.In amazon ec2 dashboard choose launch instance.
4.Select the type of images from amazon machine images list (AMI) which serves as templates for your instance.
5.On choose an Instance page, choose the desired instance from the list of instances and click review and launch.
6.Next click launch.
7.Select the existing key pair or create your own key pair to to restrict access of the instnace you just created.
8.Then click launch instances.
